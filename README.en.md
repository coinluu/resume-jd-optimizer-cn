## What It Does

This project does not simply polish resume wording. It:

1. Parses a target job description.
2. Maps every important requirement to verified resume evidence.
3. Identifies missing or weak evidence.
4. Asks focused questions before rewriting.
5. Produces a JD-specific resume and ATS-friendly plain-text version.
6. Reviews the result from ATS, recruiter, interviewer, and credibility perspectives.

## Core Principles

- Never invent experience, tools, certificates, or metrics.
- Never turn participation into ownership without evidence.
- Never keyword-stuff for ATS.
- Never present transferable experience as direct experience.
- Every rewritten statement should survive interview follow-up questions.

## Quick Start

```text
Use resume-jd-optimizer-cn to optimize my Chinese resume.

Target job description:
[paste JD]

Current resume:
[paste resume]

Additional context:
[career change, portfolio, verified metrics, application feedback]

Analyze the gaps first. Ask questions when evidence is missing. Do not invent facts.
```

## Outputs

- JD-to-resume evidence map
- Gap diagnosis and focused follow-up questions
- JD-specific Chinese resume
- ATS-friendly plain-text resume
- Recruiter summary and Boss Zhipin opening message
- Interview follow-up questions and credibility risks

See [installation instructions](docs/installation.md). Product availability may vary by account and workspace.

## Safety and Scope

This project does not guarantee ATS passage, interviews, or job offers. Scores describe the current resume material, not hiring probability.

Do not submit personal contact details, identity numbers, confidential customer information, or unpublished business data to public issues.
